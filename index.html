LIFE OF ROBERT BALD

Father of Mining Engineering (1776 -1861)

Year of Birth

1776

Place of Birth

Scotland

Educational background

  • Apprenticeship: at 14, Bald began an apprenticeship with a local blacksmith,gaining practical skills
  • University of Glasgow: attended lectures on chemistry and minerology
  • Practical training: recieved hands-on training in minning and engineering through work experience and self-study

Career Highlights

  1. Manager, Leadhills Mining Company (1801-1834)
  2. Introduced innovative mining techniques
  3. Improved safety standards
  4. Developed new machinery
  5. Wrote influencial mining texts, including "A General View of the Coal trade of Scotland"(1812)
